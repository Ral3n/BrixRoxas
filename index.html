Our Story

Born from fire and
the perfect pour.

Brix's started as an obsession — with marbling, with smoke, with the science of a perfect espresso extraction. Tucked into Banica along the river road of Roxas City, we built a place for people who don't do things halfway.

Every cut is sourced from Capiz's finest cattle farms and aged in-house. Every coffee is roasted to order, pulled at exactly the pressure and temperature the bean demands. Nothing here is accidental.
